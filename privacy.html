Privacy Policy

Short version: this site doesn't collect anything about you. Here's the full breakdown.

What data is collected

This site has no forms, no chat, and no accounts — so there's nothing for you to submit. The only data involved is standard web server logs generated automatically by any website:

  • IP address, browser type, and pages requested — logged briefly for uptime and security monitoring
  • No names, emails, or identifying details are requested or stored anywhere on this site

How data is used

Server logs exist purely to keep the site running and secure — spotting outages, abuse, or broken pages. They are not analyzed for behavior tracking, not sold, and not shared with advertisers.

Third parties

A couple of external services are used to load design assets. Visiting this site sends a request to:

  • Google Fonts — serves the Inter typeface (subject to Google's own privacy policy)
  • jsDelivr — serves open-source icon assets used in the Skills section

No analytics, advertising, or tracking scripts are used. Hosting runs on personal infrastructure behind a self-managed reverse proxy — not a third-party analytics platform.

Cookies and local storage

No cookies are set by this site. Browser localStorage is used for exactly one thing: remembering whether you prefer light or dark mode on this device. That preference never leaves your browser.

No user accounts

There's no login, registration, or account system anywhere on this site — so there's no password or profile data to protect in the first place.
